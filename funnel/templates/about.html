funnel cake

This program is aimed to help combine multiple Spotify Playlists into a larger one.

Funnel Cake needs access to the following to work correctly:

  1. playlist-modify-public
  2. user-library-read
  3. user-library-modify
  4. user-read-email
  5. user-read-private

Reasons why these permissions are needed, please read the documentation here for full transparency

usage

  1. Click authenticate in the top left hand corner
  2. Give funnel cake the proper permissions
  3. Plug in two playlist urls you wish to merge
  4. Give the merged playlist a name and click the button labeled "Merge"
  5. Profit
